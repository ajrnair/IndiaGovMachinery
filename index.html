Machinery of Government — India

An interactive map of Indian central government — showing how ministries, ministers, departments, agencies, public bodies, and other organisations relate to one another.

Navigate: Scroll to zoom, click and drag to pan. Click any node to see details. Use Search to find entities.

Views: Full view shows every entity on concentric rings by constitutional distance from the PM. Focus view shows the selected entity at the centre with its parents and children.

Legend: Circles = Officials, Squares = Departments, Diamonds = Public Bodies, Triangles = Judiciary, Hexagons = Legislature.

Budget: Click the ₹ Budget button to see a Sankey flow diagram showing how the Union Budget flows from total expenditure through sectors to individual ministries.

Data: 53 ministries, 40+ statutory bodies, 60+ autonomous bodies, 50 PSUs, 25 High Courts, 10 tribunals, 43 Ministers of State. Budget data from Union Budget 2025-26.

Data reflects the Indian government structure as of early 2025. Built with vanilla JS, no external dependencies. Inspired by machineryofgovernment.uk.
